How To Transfer Money Online?

  1. Login to your mobile/internet banking account.
  2. On the main page that appears, click on 'Fund Transfer' option.
  3. Select 'IMPS' as your method of fund transfer.
  4. Provide the beneficiary's MMID and your MPIN to proceed.
  5. Enter the amount that needs to be transferred.
  6. Click on 'Confirm'
